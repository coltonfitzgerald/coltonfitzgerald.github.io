9th Grade

Self Portrait
Self Portrait Project

We were required to take a picture of ourselves, and make a self portrait on illustrator based off of that picture. I used what we learned about the pen tool basically the entire time on this project. The entire body was made with the pen tool, including everything but the background and the hair. For the hair I used a paint brush and made a bunch of small strokes over an object that I made with the pen tool. i also changed the colors up a bunch to make the picture a little more fun.

Logo Project

Each student in the class was required to make a personal logo including either their initials, full name, or first name using Illustrator. We also had to make a PowerPoint presentation to go with it. Instead of using Powerpoint I made my presentation using Prezi. For the logo, I made the entire thing in Illustrator (so I could resize it as a I please) and made a simple circle with my initials in the middle. I then changed the opacity around and played with fonts until I got the combination that I liked.
